# Influensure 🚀

### AI-Powered Influencer Discovery & Fraud Detection Platform

Influensure is a smart influencer marketing platform designed to help brands discover authentic creators, detect fake engagement, generate campaign briefs, and maximize campaign ROI. The platform solves one of the biggest challenges in influencer marketing: identifying trustworthy influencers and avoiding marketing budget losses caused by fake followers and bot engagement.

---

## Problem Statement

Brands spend significant time and money selecting influencers for marketing campaigns. However:

* Fake followers and bot engagement distort influencer performance metrics.
* Marketing teams spend hours manually researching creators.
* Brands often collaborate with influencers who fail to deliver expected results.
* Poor influencer selection leads to wasted campaign budgets and low ROI.

Influensure addresses these challenges through AI-driven influencer discovery, fraud detection, and campaign analytics.
